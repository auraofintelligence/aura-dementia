Aura of Intelligence is a compassionate digital companion for individuals with dementia. We use advanced AI to create a personalized "digital twin" that preserves identity, supports daily life, and strengthens the bonds between loved ones and caregivers.

Discover More

The Core of the Aura

Our system is built on a foundation of deep, person-centered AI to create a truly holistic digital reflection of an individual.

Persona Mapping

We build a rich, digital memoir by collecting life stories, values, and preferences through guided conversations. This process captures the essence of the individual, ensuring the AI's support is always grounded in their unique history.

Behavior Modeling

The system learns daily routines and recognizes behavioral patterns. This allows it to identify potential triggers for distress and proactively suggest calming interventions, such as playing a favorite song or showing a cherished family photo.

Social Connection

Aura visualizes the individual's "Circles of Support," facilitating moment sharing and communication. Family and friends can contribute memories, creating a collaborative tapestry that strengthens bonds and combats isolation.

We are committed to a rigorous, evidence-based path to bring Aura of Intelligence into the Australian healthcare system. Our roadmap includes phased clinical trials in Queensland, aiming for TGA approval and integration with My Aged Care and NDIS as a recognized Assistive Technology.
