You Are Not Alone
A Partner in Your Care Journey

Supporting a loved one with dementia is a journey of immense love, strength, and challenge. Aura of Intelligence is designed to be your steadfast companion, providing tools that foster connection, offer peace of mind, and empower you as a caregiver.

How Aura Supports You

We've built Aura with the entire "Circle of Support" in mind, offering tangible benefits for families, friends, and professional caregivers.

Peace of Mind

With features like automated reminders, safety alerts, and insights into your loved one's well-being, you can feel more secure. Aura's proactive behavior modeling can help de-escalate potential distress, creating a calmer home environment for everyone.

Deeper Connection

Aura is a bridge to your loved one's world. By contributing photos and stories to their digital memoir, you actively participate in reminiscence therapy, creating shared moments of joy and understanding. It’s a new way to communicate and strengthen your bond.

A Shared Journey

The Memory Mapping App isn't just for one person. With 'Duo' and 'Group' modes, you can interview your loved one, or host a family reminiscence session. Aura facilitates these precious conversations, capturing stories and strengthening the collective family narrative for generations to come.

Empowerment for Caregivers

For professional caregivers, Aura provides instant access to a rich, holistic profile, enabling truly personalized care. Understand a client's preferences, triggers, and life story from day one. It's a powerful tool for building rapport and delivering care that honors the individual.
